# Free Sample vs Paid Launch Kit

## Free sample

The free sample gives you enough to understand the setup philosophy and inspect the quality of the material.

Included:

- private AI assistant starter path;
- Telegram setup checklist;
- basic `AGENTS.md` template;
- basic `SOUL.md` template;
- paid kit preview.

Best for:

- checking whether the style is useful;
- understanding the recommended first path;
- avoiding a few common Telegram mistakes;
- deciding whether the full kit is worth buying.

---

## Paid Launch Kit

The paid Launch Kit is the full self-serve package.

Included:

- complete quick start;
- PC deployment guide;
- VPS deployment guide;
- local models guide;
- model choice guide;
- full Telegram setup guide;
- personality/template guide;
- troubleshooting guide;
- recommended starter builds;
- worked example;
- complete template pack;
- persona overlays;
- launch/security/Telegram checklists;
- HTML release pack.

Best for:

- getting to a working private Telegram assistant faster;
- reducing setup trial-and-error;
- choosing a sane model/deployment path;
- configuring Telegram correctly;
- making the assistant feel more personal and useful;
- having a clear troubleshooting path when something breaks.

---

## Simple decision

Use the free sample if you are only exploring.

Buy the full kit if you already know you want to build a private OpenClaw assistant and would rather follow a structured path than figure out every layer from scattered docs.
